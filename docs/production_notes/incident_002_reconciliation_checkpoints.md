# Note 002: Double-Checking Every Email

### 💡 The Problem
What happens if you open a high-priority "Placement" or "Interview" email on your phone *before* the bot has a chance to see it? 

Normally, once an email is "read," many bots skip over it. This meant that if you were fast, the bot might miss an important email and forget to label it or save it to your records.

### ✅ The Solution: The "Double-Check" System
The bot now uses a smarter search strategy. It doesn't just look for "unread" mail; it also keeps track of the **exact time** it last checked your inbox.

Every time it runs, it checks:
1. Everything currently **Unread**.
2. **Anything New** that arrived since its last check—even if you've already opened it.

The bot is smart enough to recognize if it has already seen an email, so it won't process the same one twice.

### 📈 Result
Zero missed emails. You can check your phone as much as you want! Even if you open an email first, the bot will still find it, classify it, and label it for you in the background.
